Reducing Time Spent on Low-Impact Work

The Problem:

Managers spend 30% of their time on low-impact work. Refer to the McKinsey "Middle Managers (your most valuable resource)" and Gallup "The cost of disengaged staff" articles for more information.

Illustrative Example:

Assume a manager's annual salary cost is R600,000. 30% of their time = R180,000 wasted on low-impact work. A 20% reduction in wasted time = R36,000 saved per manager per year.

The Impact:

Across 25 managers, this results in R900,000 saved per year.

The Problem:

The Impact:

Across 25 managers, this results in R1.05 million saved per year in recovered productivity.
