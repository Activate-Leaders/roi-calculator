How We Calculate ROI

Calculating cost-savings for time spent on low-impact work:

Estimated Average Manager Salary at SoluGrowth: R600,000 per year (~R50,000 per month)

Time Spent on Low-Impact Tasks: McKinsey Insights

Targeted Reduction in Low-Impact Work: 20% reduction in time spent on low-value tasks.

20% of R240,000 = R48,000 saved per manager per year.

Company-Wide Gains: Across 25 managers, total savings from reducing time spent on low-impact work = R1.2 million per year in reduced time wasted.

Calculating cost-savings for time spent on unnecessary oversight:

Estimated Average Manager Salary at SoluGrowth: R600,000 per year (~R50,000 per month)

Time Spent on Task Oversight: McKinsey Insights

Targeted Reduction in Oversight: 20% improvement in team execution means managers spend 20% less time on oversight.

20% of R210,000 = R42,000 saved per manager per year.

Company-Wide Gains: Across 25 managers, total savings from improved team execution results in R1 million saved per year in recovered productivity. (R42,000 × 25 managers)

Calculating cost-savings on reducing employee turnover:

Estimated Average Employee Salary at SoluGrowth: R300,000 per year (~R25,000 per month)

Turnover Risk Due to Lack of Structured Development: Employees currently without structured development plans: 59% of 250 employees = 148 employees

Estimated turnover risk for these employees: Research suggests employees without clear career paths are more likely to leave. Assume 10% annual staff turnover. (HR Insights)

Estimated number of employees lost annually due to lack of structured growth: 148 × 10% = 15 employees per year.

Estimated cost of replacing an employee: 50% of salary (R150,000 per lost employee). (Gallup Insights)

Total turnover cost due to lack of structured development: 15 × R150,000 = R2.25 million per year.

Targeted Reduction in Turnover Due to Structured Development: New number of employees without a plan: 30% of 250 employees = 75 employees

Estimated retention improvement: 25% reduction in turnover risk.

Employees retained due to structured career development: (148 - 75) × 10% = 7 employees retained per year.

7 × R150,000 = R1 million prevented cost per year.
